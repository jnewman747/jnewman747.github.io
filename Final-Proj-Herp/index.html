Herpetology Lab at the University of Kentucky


Price's Projects (2013-Present)

Dr. Steve Price is a professor within the Forestry and Natural Resources Department at the University of Kentucky. He earned his B.S. (2000) and M.S. (2003) from the University of Wisconsin-Green Bay and his PhD from Wake Forest (2011). He was employed at Davidson College as a Research Coordinator, faculty, and post-doc from 2004-2012. His research focuses on ecology and conservation of aquatic and semi-aquatic organisms (particularly reptiles and amphibians) in Kentucky and its surrounding states.
