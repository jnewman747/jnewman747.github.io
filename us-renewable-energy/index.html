US Power Plants


sources of electricity

Sources of electricity can be split into three categories: fossil fuels, nuclear, and renewable energy. The above interactive map highlights power plants for three types of renewable energy sources in the United States: hydro, wind, and solar. Double click on the map to learn more about the power plants. Use the map legend to toggle on and off between these three layers. Data from this map was collected here .
